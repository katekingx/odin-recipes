Five Dollar Shake

You can use my straw, I don't have cooties...

Ingredients

  • 2 peeled frozen bananas
  • 300g vanilla ice cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 21 tbsp maple syrup
  • 150ml vanilla oat milk
  • 1 cup ice
  • canned whip cream(for topping)
  • maraschino cherries(for topping)

Steps

  1. Add the bananas, ice cream, vanilla extract, maple syrup, oat milk, and ice to a high powered blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour milkshake into 2 glasses and top with whipcream and maraschino cherries.
